Simon Fraser Career is the career department of Simon Fraser University. Big Fair Documentary is the conclusion of the video sets, which includes SAP, Sky Box Lab, Stemcell Technology and Camp Pacific. The purpose of those four documentaries is promoting the company in the big fair event. The companies are using those video to introduce themselves to the students. I work with my club members in this project. I am the vice president of the Simon Fraser Cinematography. In this project, I take a role as the director, cameraman, editor and light guider in this project.


After accepting the project from SFU Career we have several meeting with both SFU crews and the company representative, which make sure our storyboard and interview question is perfectly reasonable and preservative. After that, we took almost 3 days filming the interview and the B-roll. I spent 2 weeks on editing the footage, which including rough cut, colour grading and final delivery. During the editing process, we meet the company represents several times to confirm the material.

A Follower is one of my projects in class. It is showing a horror story between the follower and the victim. The project wins the best sound effect among 20+ projects in class. I take a role as the director, cameraman, Editor and light guider in A FOLLOWER. It takes me around two days on flaming and recording the footage. I spend the rest of the time on editing that footage when I am having class at school.

This project is a lecture assignment project. I work with my teammate during the whole project. We brainstormed and discussed the story before we filming the footage. After that, basically, I did the whole post-production. The premiere is my working platform and DaVinci is the colour grading assistants.
